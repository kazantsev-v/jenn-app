Philosophy of Digital Architecture

Architecture is not merely about physical structures; it is about the spaces where human interaction flourishes. In the digital realm, this manifests as the interface—the bridge between consciousness and silicon.

[[Spatial Informatics]] refers to the way we map conceptual nodes to physical-like environments.

When knowledge is atomized into blocks, the relationships between these atoms form a higher-order structure: the Knowledge Graph.
